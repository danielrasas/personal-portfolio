Physics 2024 Education

Physics Demonstration Video

A visual exploration of core physics principles through the lens of skiing.

Project Description

This project focuses on identifying complex engineering and physics problems, clearly communicating solutions, and implementing improvements. Using skiing as a dynamic medium, I demonstrate core physics concepts such as friction, momentum, and inertia. By creating physical analogies, the video makes abstract principles accessible and engaging for learners of all levels.
