Mechanical Engineering 2023 Design

3D Printed Gear System

3D printed gift for an amazing teacher, with a gear system opening a interior compartment (containing a thank-you note)

Project Data

Duration

2 Weeks

Tools Used

Fusion 360, UltiMaker Cura

Inspiration

"Gedimino Pilis is a famous national landmark in Lithuania, so I thought it would be the perfect gift for my Lithuanian teacher"

Objective

The primary goal was to design a gear system capable of transmitting high torque while minimizing weight and wear.

Mechanism Design

We utilized advanced CAD modeling and Finite Element Analysis (FEA) to ensure structural integrity under load.

"The design achieved a 15% weight reduction while maintaining a safety factor of 2.0."

Engineering Challenges

The gear tolerances were not quite right, so the gears were pretty stiff. After lots of sanding, the mechanism works.
